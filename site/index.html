fnsvr

Your K-1 is buried in your third inbox.

A local CLI tool that scans your Gmail accounts for financial emails you cannot afford to miss. Read-only. Runs on your machine. Nothing leaves.

View on GitHub

The problem

You have three Gmail accounts. Maybe five. Financial emails land across all of them: K-1s from your partnership, a DocuSign for an equity agreement, a wire confirmation from your bank. They sit between newsletters and promo blasts.

Unlike a missed meeting, a missed K-1 or unsigned equity doc has real dollar consequences. Gmail filters do not work across accounts, cannot prioritize by financial urgency, and create no audit trail.

How it works

1
Install
pip install fnsvr
2
Scan
Checks all your Gmail accounts. Read-only, local-only.
3
Review
Notifications, digests, and a terminal review queue.

How it's built

  • Local-first, always. Your email data never leaves your machine. No cloud, no accounts, no SaaS.
  • Read-only by design. The Gmail scope is gmail.readonly. Architecturally incapable of touching your inbox.
  • Config over code. Add a new bank or broker with a one-line YAML edit. No code changes needed.
  • Open source. MIT licensed. Audit the code, extend the patterns, own the tool.

Get started

$ pip install fnsvr
$ fnsvr init
$ fnsvr setup personal
$ fnsvr scan --initial

Scans the last 90 days across all configured accounts.
